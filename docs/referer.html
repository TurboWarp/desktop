TurboWarp Desktop Referer

If you're seeing requests to your website with this page in its Referer header, that probably means someone is using a program they created in TurboWarp Desktop to interact with your website.

Per our privacy policy, we do not track our users and can't tell you who is accessing your website or why.

Questions or concerns? You can email contact at turbowarp dot org, but again, TurboWarp Desktop is just a programming environment. We aren't the people sending requests to your server.
